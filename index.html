Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

We take care of all pets! Dogs, cats, horses, goats, chickens, rabbits, snakes, and many other different pets.
We strive to provide consistency in pet care by assigning the same sitter to your pets whenever possible. However, in some cases, depending on your pet care needs, it may be necessary to assign two or more sitters. Rest assured that each sitter assigned to care for your pet will have access to your pet care instructions to ensure that your pet(s) receive the same level of loving care every time.
At The Purr-fect Paw, safety and security are paramount. We conduct background checks on all of our sitters, and provide insurance and bonding for added protection. Additionally, our sitters must complete our Certified Pet Sitter program before being entrusted with the care of your pets. Our rigorous screening process ensures that you can trust us to provide reliable and trustworthy pet care services.
At The Purr-fect Paw, we take pet care seriously. We partner with a reputable third-party company called FetchFind. Our sitters undergo extensive training, watching 12–15 hours of educational material before they begin their pet sitting duties. The courses cover feline fundamentals, canine behavior, communication, bite prevention, canine health and more.
If you live outside our service area, there is a Gas Surcharge of $0.25 per mile. We do not provide pet care if you live more than 3 miles outside our service area.
To ensure security, we do not retain keys. During the first consultation, we provide the option to purchase a lockbox from us for $15, or you can provide your own. On the day of departure, you will leave the lockbox at a location of your choice and assign a four-digit combination. If you have a garage or door keypad or any secure place to hide your key, a lockbox is unnecessary. We do not accept garage openers, and we do not hold keys in our office for your protection.
We use Time to Pet, a software that allows our sitters to scan in with the app once they arrive at your home. Upon the departure of every appointment, you'll receive an email/text alert with a detailed report of the visit and lots of photos of your pets.
Certainly! Our sitters are experienced and trained to administer almost any type of medication to your pets, provided that they are willing to take it. If you have concerns that your pet may resist taking their medication, please let us know so that we can work with you to find an alternative solution.
While our sitters are well-trained in understanding a dog's body language, we prioritize the safety and well-being of both your pet and our sitters. We are unable to take on aggressive or extremely skittish dogs. During the initial consultation, we will assess your dog's behavior and provide our professional opinion. The majority of dogs do fantastic.
In the rare event of an emergency, your pet's safety is our top priority. We will contact you right away and take all appropriate steps to protect your pets and home. If we are unable to reach you, we will act in your pet's best interest using our best judgment. Unless the emergency was caused by our actions, any associated costs will be your responsibility.
The earliest we arrive is between 7:00 AM – 9:00 AM, and the latest timeframe is between 8:00 PM – 10:00 PM. We can arrive between 6:00 AM – 7:00 AM or after 10:00 PM for an additional $10 per visit. With your online account, you will be able to schedule services in whatever time frames you prefer.
There is a $20 charge for same-day and a $15 charge for next-day requests. We do not guarantee that we can accommodate any last-minute request as this will depend on sitter availability. If you are a new client, we must schedule a meet & greet 3 days before your first scheduled visit. Once you are in our system we guarantee availability if it's at least 2 days before your first scheduled visit.
We will NEVER charge you more for having multiple pets! We only charge for the amount of time our sitters spend taking care of your furry family.
Minor Holiday Surcharge – Easter Sunday, 4th of July, Memorial Day, Labor Day: $10 per visit. Major Holiday Surcharge – Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve & Christmas Day: $20 per visit.
Yes, we offer 3 different discounts!

Single Pet Discount – Only have 1 pet? Take 10% off your entire invoice!

Daily Discount – When scheduling 10+ visits in a single month, receive 5% off your invoice!

Cash Discount – We also offer a 5% discount when you pay in cash.
